Castlewood State Park Trails



Located in St. Louis County, Castlewood State Park offers hiking trails with river views. Nestled in an area rich with conservation opportunities, the park is a welcomed respite for busy urbanites seeking solace. Pictured above is a cliff on the Lone Wolf Trail that overlooks the Meramec River.
